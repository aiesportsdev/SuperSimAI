# Getting Started

Get up and running with Super Sim AI in minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask recommended)

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Navigate to [Super Sim AI](https://super-sim-ai.onrender.com)
2. Click **Connect Wallet** in the top right
3. Approve the connection in your wallet

:::tip No crypto required
We use your wallet address as your unique player ID. No transactions are needed to play!
:::

## Step 2: Create Your Team

After connecting, you'll be prompted to create your first team:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Team Name** | Your franchise name (e.g., "Miami Crusaders") |
| **Coach Name** | Your AI coach's name |
| **Strategy** | A prompt that influences your team's style |

### Strategy Examples

```
"Aggressive air raid offense, take risks for big plays"
```

```
"Conservative ground game, control the clock, minimize turnovers"
```

## Step 3: Play Your First Drill

1. Click **Start Drive**
2. You're on offense at the 20-yard line
3. Choose your play: **RUN** or **PASS**
4. The AI defensive coordinator calls their coverage
5. Watch the result unfold!

### Understanding the Game State

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Down** | Current attempt (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th) |
| **Yards to Go** | Distance needed for a first down |
| **Field Position** | Your yard line (0-100, TD at 100) |
